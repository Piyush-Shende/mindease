About MindEase

MindEase is an AI-powered daily wellness check-in companion designed to support emotional awareness and general well-being through thoughtful conversation.

AI Companion Powered by Google Gemini with carefully crafted prompt engineering for empathetic, non-medical support.
Mood Tracking Track your emotional journey throughout the week with visual mood indicators.
Stress Monitoring Log your daily stress level and receive personalized coping suggestions.
Wellness Tools Guided breathing exercises, daily affirmations, and a personal journal.
Built With
HTML/CSS/JS Python Flask Google Gemini API Prompt Engineering
Important Notice

MindEase is not a substitute for professional mental health care. It does not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice. If you are in crisis, please contact emergency services or a helpline like 988 (Suicide and Crisis Lifeline).
